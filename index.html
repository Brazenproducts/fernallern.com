About the Author

Fern Allern

Fern Allern writes dark fae romance that lives in the thorny space between longing and danger. She has always been drawn to stories where love is as wild and unpredictable as the woods themselves — stories where the monster might be the one worth trusting.

The Thornwood Accord Trilogy is her debut series: a slow-burn enemies-to-lovers fae romance with all the bargains, courts, and forbidden magic you could want.

When not writing, Fern can be found reading romantasy, drinking too much tea, and having deeply personal conversations with her houseplants.
